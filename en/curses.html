FP Curses

In this section we perform all courses at our academy are described.

  • Graphic design

    • Basic

      In this course terms and methods of "art" and simple skills they are taught photographer. It will be taught to use programs such as Photoshop or Lightroom and static graphics. This course lasts two years, enrollment of € 100 and € 65 / month.

    • Advanced

      Unlike the basic knowledge here last year and apply new dynamic graphic design and moving. Taught to use complex and advanced programs such as Cinema 4D or Unity 3D , in addition to Maya to design sketches itself. This course takes two years to full and must make a mid-level to access the same, registration 120 € and 80 € / month.

  • Video editing

    • Basic

      In this course techniques of video and film are taught in addition to learning about the use of programs such as Sony Vegas , Pinnacle Studio and Premiere . And thanks to partner companies as SONY and GameChangerFilms we can make an extra course on making short films and trailers. This course lasts two years, enrollment of € 100 and € 65 / month. No entrance examination is necessary.

    • Advanced

      This course is more focused on the development of proprietary source code, that is, has been much development and resources as effects, custom animations and transitions for later import to video editing programs we use are created. 2015 As a new subject, animation programs like Cinema 4D and Unity 3D will be held. This course lasts two years, enrollment 120 € and 80 € / month.

  • Audio editing

    • Basic

      This course teaches sound knowledge and know edit and create audio waves or sound spectra. This course lasts one years, tuition 70 € and 50 € / month.

    • Advanced

      Unlike the basic course are taught to use mixing tables, thanks to the partner company Pioneer have material quality support and help. This course lasts 1 year and not require an entrance examination, registration 90 € and 60 € / month.

  • Videogame programming

    • " This is the most popular course, so there are no places for everyone, be sure to send your registration before June 11 so we can take your request into account. "

    • Basic

      In this course theoretical and practical knowledge about video games and programs used to create them are taught, such as; Unity 3D , Corona SDK (optional "Mobile Application Development"), Unreal Development Kit , Maya as texturing, and Cinema 4D as 3D sketch animations games.

      This course lasts two years and is necessary middleweight title cycle "microcomputer system and networks" (or quivalente) plus an aptitude test in the month of July (more details) , registration 200 € and 120 € / month.

      - Aptitude test: Day July 17 at our academy, from 10: 00h to 13: 00h, and ID required prior contact.

    • Advanced

      In this video game programming course I is fully focused on programming, the source code of the movements and actions. Lasts for an entire two years, enrollment 250 € and 140 € / month.

  • Web programming

    • Basic

      This course teaches programming language HTML and CSS in versions HTML5 and CSS3 . This course lasts two years, enrollment of € 100 and € 70 / month.

    • Advanced

      This course lasts 3 years, highly advanced programming HTML5 , CSS3 is taught with a small basic extra course of CSS4 (currently in Beta) Javascript (and jQuery ), AJAX and PHP . Test access to it is required, in which a requirement is a grade point average of 7 in the intermediate year. Registration of € 160 and € 90 / month.
